Gymate - Gym Fitness Bootstrap 5 Template

Gymate is an elegant and beautiful Gym Fitness Bootstrap 5 Template. It is 100% responsive and mobile-friendly. Gymate is best suited for making any type of fitness center or fitness training website. It comes with 8 main pages. They include the home page, about page, team page, contact page, our program page, shop page, blog page, and event page.
The home page includes a section for displaying various fitness-related services. It also has a section for displaying training coaches or training instructors. The home page also has a section for calculating your BMI.
The about page contains a text section for displayingcontent related to the fitness training center. Our team page includes a section for displaying different training coaches or different fitness training instructors
The contact page includes an embedded Google Map for displaying your fitness center location as well as a form for receiving messages or queries from your clients.
Our program page contains a schedule of different upcoming and past events divided into various categories. The shop page includes fitness related items for sale. The blog page has a grid display all the fitness-related blogs. The blog page also has a blog details page for showing a particular blog in detail. The event page contains a grid of various past and upcoming events
